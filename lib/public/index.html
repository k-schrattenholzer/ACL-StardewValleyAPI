A RESTful API for Stardew Valley info.

Current Routes

    Characters

  • /api/v1/characters
  • /api/v1/characters/:id

  • Example Request:

    https://stardewvalley-api.herokuapp.com/api/v1/characters/1

  • Example Response:

    { "id": "1", "name": "Krobus", "birthday": "Winter 1", "address": "Krobus\" shop", "elligible": "false", "img": "https://stardewvalleywiki.com/mediawiki/images/7/71/Krobus.png", "best_gifts": [ "Diamond", "Iridium Bar", "Pumpkin", "Void Egg", "Void Mayonnaise", "Wild Horseradish" ], "about": "Krobus is the only friendly monster players will encounter, however he still refers to other hostile monsters as his friends. He is a shadow person who lives in the sewers. He sells a variety of rare goods. He is also available as a roommate." }
